About Twenty One AI

Founded in 2009, Twenty One AI Automation Agency was born from a simple vision: to democratize artificial intelligence and automation technology for businesses of all sizes. Our founding team of data scientists, software engineers, and business strategists recognized that most organizations struggled to unlock the transformative potential of AI due to complexity, cost, and lack of expertise. We set out to change that by creating accessible, practical, and powerful automation solutions that deliver measurable results. Over the past 15 years, we've grown from a small startup to a trusted partner for 500+ organizations globally, helping them reimagine their operations through intelligent automation.

Our mission is to empower businesses to achieve digital transformation through innovative AI automation solutions that are both intelligent and human-centered. We believe that technology should enhance human capabilities, not replace them. Our core values—Innovation, Integrity, Impact, and Inclusion—guide every project we undertake. We're committed to staying at the forefront of AI and automation technology, continuously learning and adapting to serve our clients better. We invest heavily in research and development, maintain certifications with leading technology providers, and foster a culture of continuous improvement. At Twenty One AI, success isn't just measured in metrics—it's measured in the positive impact we create for our clients, their employees, and the broader business community.
